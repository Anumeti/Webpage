What is DarkWeb?

17 Jan 2024
|
Online Surffing

The "dark web" refers to parts of the internet that aren't indexed by traditional search engines and require specific tools, configurations, or authorization to access. It is often associated with anonymity and privacy due to its reliance on technologies like Tor (The Onion Router) or I2P (Invisible Internet Project).
